VALOPEDIA

Weapons are princpal to VALORANT. Combined with ability use, players eliminate opponents to win rounds using weapons. Every round, players earn points which enable them to buy weapons. Maintaining good economy every round is essential to be able to afford weapons of choice. Tactical choices need to be made to maintain economy.





Different Types of Weapons




MELEE

The Tactical Knife that every player is equipped with by default, it cannot be dropped. There are two motions players can do with the knife: slash and jab.




SIDEARMS

Sidearms are pistols that can be operated with a single arm. They are effective short-range weapons. Sidearm weapons in VALORANT include the Classic, the Frenzy, the Shorty, the Ghost, and the Sheriff. The Classic is provided to players by default, it is the only free gun in the game.




SMGS

SMGs are low-cost weapons good for short-range or on-the-move firing. They have low magazine capacity but good firing rate. The Spectre and the Stinger are the SMGs in VALORANT. The former being a more popular choice than the latter.




SHOTGUNS

Shotguns are great for clearing small areas but useless at long range due to pellet spread. They have low firing rate and capacity. Bucky and Judge are the shotguns used in VALORANT. Bucky is the cheapest primary weapon in the game.




RIFLES

Automatic and semi-automatic are excellent weapons for single shot kills. VALORANT has four rifles. The Phantom and the Bulldog are good for short to medium range fights due to their less scattered spray pattern and the Vandal and Guardian being better choices for long range fights.




SNIPERS

Snipers are high-cost weapons with the best long-range capabilities. The high power of the weapons allows single-shot kills, though the downside is that the reload rate is extremely low. The Operator and the Marshal are the snipers used in VALORANT. Missing a shot with a sniper can come with a terrible cost.




MACHINE GUNS

Machine guns have extremely high power and firing rates, allowing bullet penetration through doors and walls. These weapons allow low mobility but have high equip speeds. The Odin and The Ares are most sought weapons in the game, best used defensively.
