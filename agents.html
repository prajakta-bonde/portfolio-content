Agents are playable characters in the game, each from on a different culture or country of the world. Each agent has unique abilities. The type of ability an Agent has is decided by the class they belong to. There are four main classes or roles of Agents in Valorant. The playstyle and ability of an Agent is decided by their class.

Agent Roles

Controller

Controllers specialize in "slicing up dangerous territory to set their team up for success."[25] They use their abilities to create coverage or clear out areas of space with crowd control. To help their team enter into enemy territory, their abilities consist of some kind of smoke, as well as molotovs, stuns, or flashes. With their smokes, controllers can control sightlines on the map, making it safer to move through the map without getting seen.

Duelist

Duelists specialize in attacking and entering a bomb site for the team. Duelists mainly create space for their team while entering onto a site, giving their teammates information, and making entering a site easier. Their abilities tend to consist of flashes which blind enemies, and movement-based abilities that allow for them to cover large distances faster than other agents.

Initiator

Initiators plan out the offensive pushes. Initiators specialize in breaking through defensive enemy positions. Initiators' abilities can consist of flashes but also abilities that can reveal the location of enemies. This information allows for attackers to know where enemies are and make taking a site easier.

Sentinal

Sentinels are the defensive line, which specializes in locking down sites and protecting teammates from enemies. Their abilities mainly consist of static 'objects' that are obstacles to the enemies. These objects can give the team valuable information and/or deal damage. On attack, sentinels can use their abilities to cut off certain parts of the map or set up 'objects' that can ensure the enemy cannot flank without being noticed.

Agents of Valorant Protocol

Brimstone

Liam Byrne aka Brimstone from USA is the first agent recruited into the Valorant Protocol. As the seniormost member, he is also the commander of VALORANT. Brimstone is a Controller and his abilities are the Stim Beacon, Incendiary, Sky Smoke and Orbital Strike (his ultimate ability).

Breach

Erik Torsten aka Breach is the thirteenth agent recruited into the Valorant Protocol. Breach left a life of crime in Sweden to serve the Protocol. With his two mechanical arms, Breach plays the role of Initiator with his abilities Aftershock, Falshpoint, Fault Line and his ultimate, Rolling Thunder.

Cypher

Amir el Amari from Morocco is an information broker who joined the Valorant Protocol as its fifth agent to fight the Kingdom Corporation. Cypher harbors information, only to give it away at a price. He is a Sentinal with intel-gathering abilities like his Spy Cam, Trap Wire, Cyber Cage, Neural Theft.

Jett

South Korean chef Sunwoo Han possesses radiant powers which enable her to manipulate wind to give her abilities like Drift, Cloudburst, Updraft, Tailwind and Blade Storm. Jett is a powerful Duelist recruited by the Protocol as its tenth agent.

Omen

Omen is the third recruit of the Valorant Protocol. He is a phantom of a man who was involved in an incident which caused him to be ripped apart from his physical body. The third recruit to join VALORANT, Controller Omen has a frightening and mysterious appearance. His abilities include Shrouded Step, Paranoia, Dark Shadow, From The Shadows.

Phoenix

Jamie Adeyemi is another radiant who possesses powers over fire. Hailing from London, Phoenix is a confident, young Duelist who joined VALORANT as its ninth agent. He is good at adapting to the circumstances and escaping sticky situations with his ultimate ability. His abilities are Blaze, Curveball, Hot Hands, and Run it Back.
